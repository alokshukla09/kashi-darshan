Problem Statement:

Pilgrims visiting Varanasi often struggle to plan their trip because information about temples, pujas, pandits, boat rides, accommodation, transportation, and local services is scattered across multiple sources.

There is no single trusted platform that allows users to discover, compare, and book these services online.


Proposed Solution:

Kashi Darshan is a digital platform that enables devotees and tourists to plan their complete spiritual journey to Varanasi.

Users can:

Discover temples
Book Pujas
Hire Pandits
Reserve Boat Rides
Book Hotels
Hire Taxi
Pay Online
Track Bookings

through a single platform.

Target Audience:

Domestic Pilgrims
International Tourists
Senior Citizens
Travel Agencies
Temple Authorities
